Seven Algorithms--One Product

Aquanaut aggregates data from a single piece of equipment to produce seven unique outputs, which can be combined in one final deep learning application to represent the overall health of a fish farm.

Sea Lice Detection

Detect sea-lice before they spread over to the other fish. Perform interventions before critical infestation levels require a complete chemical flush for the entire farm.

Behavioral Analysis of Fish

Identify behavior anomalies associated with increased Cortisol and correlate it with recent environmental changes. Reduce stress, ensure quality-of-life, and increase meat quality.

Dynamic Fish Appetite Estimation and Delivery

Optimize feeding times and food delivery size for maximum efficiency. Prevent wasted costs and excess pollution while increasing population growth rates.
