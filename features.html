Seven Algorithms--One Product

Aquanaut aggregates data from a single piece of equipment to produce seven unique outputs, which can be combined in one final deep learning application to represent the overall health of a fish farm.

Sea Lice Detection

Detect sea-lice before they spread over to the other fish. Perform interventions before critical infestation levels require a complete chemical flush for the entire farm.

Behavioral Analysis of Fish

Identify behavior anomalies associated with increased Cortisol and correlate it with recent environmental changes. Reduce stress, ensure quality-of-life, and increase meat quality.

Dynamic Fish Appetite Estimation and Delivery

Optimize feeding times and food delivery size for maximum efficiency. Prevent wasted costs and excess pollution while increasing population growth rates.

Biomass Measurement

COMING SOON - Estimate biomass and develop insights into quantity, size, and weight of the current fish. Drive decisions related to feeding and behavioral management by leveraging live data streams.

Mesh Integrity Monitoring

COMING SOON - Prevent fish from escaping and predators from entering the farm without the need for SCUBA teams. Monitor the entire enclosure 24/7 and receive alerts when signs of net deteriation occur, before a hole fully forms.

Water Quality Monitoring and Control

Monitor and predict changes in critical parameters such as dissolved oxygen (DO) over short and long periods of time. Stabalize water quality throughout the entire farm. Reduce fish stress, increase health, and promote breeding.

Disease Identification and Prevention?

Prevent diseases from spreading by identifying sick fish and providing medical interventions faster.

Frequently asked questions

Can anyone use your models?

We make everything open source and available to the public. Advances in technology shouldn't be hidden behind paywalls and legal jargon. Feel free to make your own experiments using our products, or reach out if you want us to implement our system for you!

Will you train or optimize our models?

Yes! We will absolutely do this but with the rising costs of GPU's, this would cost money. You can see our pricing guide at:: and reach out to us for more information on which services you require.

I want to beta test your technology at my workplace, can I use any of the "Coming Soon" technologies?

Products under active-development might not be ready for field testing, but please reach out so we can talk about your use cases and get you set up for success ASAP.

Do I need to pay for your datasets?

Once a dataset has been fully curated--we make it open source (a.k.a free) so that anybody can benefit from it. If you want to utilize datasets still in production, reach out to us @

Do you believe AI will improve aquaculture and solve key problems?

Every fish farm faces a unique set of challenges based on geographical location and fish breed. Therefore, any general solution must be dynamic enough to work in an extremely robust set of environments. We believe that AI technologies leveraging deep learning can be that robust solution and any fish farm can benefit from Aquanaut!
