Humanity needs sustainable food sources and the ocean needs clean water.

The ocean has hit a breaking point. We've depleted 90% of wild fisheries, yet more than 3 billion people depend on seafood for protein. Aquanaut helps save our oceans while feeding humanity by creating sustainable fish farms that leverage deep learning solutions.
